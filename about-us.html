Why we exist

Millions of talented students in underserved communities face barriers such as lack of access, guidance, and opportunities.

Our Belief

  • Talent exists everywhere
  • Opportunity does not

Our Purpose

Sri Takshashila Gurukul exists to bridge this gap by:

  • Identifying potential in students
  • Providing structured learning and awareness programs
  • Enabling access to opportunities and mentorship

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that every deserving student is empowered with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to build a meaningful and successful future.

What We Do

Our focus areas

We run programs across education, health, women empowerment, and community awareness—all free and open to those who need them most.

Education

Free tuition, spoken English, NMMS prep, STEM, robotics, and career guidance. We also run the Takshashila Talent Scholarship and support school transformation.

Health & Wellness

Growth monitoring, addiction relief support, yoga, and Satvik Dinacharya. We believe a healthy mind and body are the foundation of learning.

Women Empowerment

Hoop Art Mastery and livelihood programs that build skills and confidence. We have given exposure and training to hundreds of women.

Awareness & Outreach

Cyber security awareness, Indian Space Show for schools, and campaigns around national and global days—building informed, responsible citizens.
